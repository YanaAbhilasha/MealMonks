INTRODUCTION


The name Mealmonks derived from two words “Meal” and “Monks” where Meal meant “food” and Monks were “The people who would provide food”. To provide food to the needy is a selfless act and we believe that it is not just food that contributes to meals, giving employment, educating, skill training and helping in setting up businesses for self employment also contributes to providing meals.



Mealmonks Foundation was started with a vision to make meals accessible for maximum people, not just food to survive but a complete meal to thrive upon.


Vision


Mealmonks Foundation was started with a vision to make meals accessible for maximum families, not just food to survive but a complete meal to thrive upon.


The poverty trap is one of the toughest circumstances to escape. Very few are able to break this trap, they are exceptional but largely they are the most helpless people having very few opportunities and resources to improve their life.


The bigger idea is to work and develop solutions at each stage of this poverty trap and create opportunities for the people to handhold them out of the poverty trap. The dark reality is that they work very hard and in the toughest and most brutal circumstances to barely survive and we want to change it for them.

Mission - The Challenge


One time effort does not make much difference in the lives of people who struggle daily to provide for their family. We have experienced this in many pilot projects, researches, activities and interaction which we did.


Therefore, We are poised to bring a long term impact and inclusive growth of the poverty stricken and resource deprived people so that they can progress out of the poverty trap and develop sustainable sources of income.


We are also actively engaged in short term projects and activities to interact and build trust of the beneficiaries. It also helps us in consistently approaching the beneficiaries which ensures them to believe in us and our efforts.
